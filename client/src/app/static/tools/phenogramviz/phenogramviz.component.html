PhenoGramViz

Clinical evaluation of CNVs identified via techniques such as array comparative genome hybridisation (aCGH) involves the inspection of lists of known and unknown duplications and deletions with the goal of distinguishing pathogenic from benign CNVs. PhenoGramViz is a tool that provides a phenotype-based approach to analyzing candidate CNVs on the basis of phenotype matching.

A key step in this process is the comparison of the individual's phenotypic abnormalities with those associated with Mendelian disorders of the genes affected by the CNV. PhenoGramViz additionally implements a comparison of model organism phenotypes with patient phenotypes in order to support the identification of novel disease genes and medical evaluation of CNVs.

  • PhenoGramViz: Automatically analyze and visualize gene-to-phenotype relations for a set of genes affected by CNV of a patient and a set of HPO-terms
