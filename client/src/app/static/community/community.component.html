This is a partial list of workshops (“hackathons”) conducted over the last decade to add content to the HPO for specific areas of medicine. Please contact the HPO team if you would like to work with us on HPO terms for an area of medicine.

  • 2017: Workshop on Ophthalmology phenotypes and rare diseases of the European Reference Network for Rare Eye Diseases
  • 2017: Workshop on cardiology at Stanford University coordinated by Matthew Wheeler
  • 2017: Workshop on Immunology terms at the National Institute for Allergy and Infectious Diseases coordinated by Morgan Similuk
  • 2015: Workshop with the UK's National Institute for Health Research (NIHR) Rare Disease initiatives on projects such as NIHR RD-TRC (Rare Disease—Translational Research Collaboration, http://rd.trc.nihr.ac.uk) coordinated by Patrick Chinnery, Mareike Veltman and Roger James.
  • 2013: Workshop on epilepsy phenotypes and EEG abnormalities led by Prof. Ingo Helbig (currently Children’s Hospital of Philadelphia, University of Pennsylvania)
  • 2012-2016: Work on bleeding phenotypes with contributions from multiple members of a team led by Prof. Willem Ouwehand of the University of Cambridge (see https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/25949529)
  • 2012: 2-day workshop in Berlin, Germany, to extend dental terminology in the HPO led by Prof. Isabelle Bailleul-Forestier (Paul Sabatier University - Toulouse, France)
  • 2009: 5-day workshop at the Sanger Institute with UK clinicians involved with the DECIPHER project
